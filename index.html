Biography

I am now a master student (year 3) from IIG group in Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Yujiu Yang. I received my bachelor degree in Department of Automation from Tsinghua University in 2020. My major research interests are NLP and KG. Recently, I focus on the weight-inherited knowledge transfer in PLM including compression and progressive training.
